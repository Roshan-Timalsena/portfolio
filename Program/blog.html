Technology in Daily Lives


In today's world, technology has become an inevitable part of human lives. We are practically surrounded by technology. I am currently writing this blog with the help of technology. In the absence of technology, our lives are useless.

From the ancient ages of crafting stone tools to the current age of crafting nano-technology, our lives have been crafted by technology. Technology has made our lives more easier and faster. Easier in a sense that, we do not have to hit two stones to light a fire- wee have matches and lighter for that matter and faster in a sense that lighting a match is way faster than hitting two stones to light a fire.

The major impact of technology can be seen in education. There are unlimited sources for study materials thrughout the internet- which is an epitome of technology. Online books and practise websites have made it easier for students to study as well as for teachers to refer the students.

Another major impact has been on the medical field. Technology has made it easier to detect any kind of disease. Surgeries and x-ray scanning are proving to be a great helping aid for doctors to cure and detect diseases. Online medical websites are also useful to get an idea of medicines for simple illness.

There are many other impacts of technology on our lives and among them education sector and health sector are most benefited by technology.
